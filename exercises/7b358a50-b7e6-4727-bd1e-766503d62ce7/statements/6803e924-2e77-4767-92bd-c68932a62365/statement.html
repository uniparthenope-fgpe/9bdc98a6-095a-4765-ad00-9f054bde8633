T-test

The t-test is a statistical test used to determine whether the means of two groups are significantly different from each other.


Write a Python function that takes two arrays of data as input and performs a two-sample t-test to determine whether the means of the two groups are significantly different from each other using the scipy.stats module.


Example input:

1 2 3 4 5

2 4 6 8 10


Example output:

(-1.8973665961010275, 0.09434977284243756)
